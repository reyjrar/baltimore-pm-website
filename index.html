The Baltimore Perl Mongers is a group of people local to the Baltimore, Maryland area with an interest in the Perl programming language. We typically meet on the third Thursday of the month. Some of us are new to the language, some are well-acquainted, some use it on UNIX, others on Windows, some personally, some professionally ... but all are welcome.
