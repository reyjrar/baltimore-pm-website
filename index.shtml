The Baltimore Perl Mongers is a group of people local to the Baltimore, Maryland area with an interest in the Perl programming language. Some of us are new to the language, some are well-acquainted, some use it on UNIX, others on Win32, but all are welcome. The group meets on the third Thursday of the month at 6:30 PM at the ETC building in Canton, 2400 Boston Street Baltimore, MD 21224 (Google Map).

Companies and recruiters who would like to post job openings in the Baltimore-area on this site are welcome to send email to (baltimorepm-owner@yahoogroups.com)

The group maintains a yahoo groups mailing list. Membership is free and encouraged. Click below to join.
